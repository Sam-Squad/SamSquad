Frequently Asked Questions

What types of devices do you support?

We support a wide range of devices including desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. Our team is experienced with Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android operating systems.

Do you offer on-site support?

Yes, we offer on-site support for businesses and home users within a 50-mile radius of our main office. For locations outside this area, we provide remote support options.

How quickly can I expect a response to my support request?

We aim to respond to all support requests within 2 hours during our business hours (9 AM - 6 PM, Monday to Friday). For emergency after-hours support, we offer a premium service with a 1-hour response time guarantee.
